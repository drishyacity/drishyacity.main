Pricing Questions

Common questions about our pricing and services

Do you offer payment plans?

For larger projects over $100, I offer flexible payment plans. Typically 50% upfront and 50% upon completion.

What's included in unlimited revisions?

Unlimited revisions means I'll keep refining your design until you're 100% satisfied. This includes color changes, layout adjustments, and typography modifications.

Do you offer rush delivery?

Yes! Rush orders are available with a 50% surcharge. Contact me to discuss your timeline and I'll let you know if I can accommodate your deadline.

What file formats do I receive?

You'll receive all necessary files including vector formats (AI, EPS, SVG) and high-resolution raster files (PNG, JPG) suitable for both print and digital use.
