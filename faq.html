Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my design services, process, and pricing.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Logo designs typically take 2-3 days, complete brand identities take 1-2 weeks, and larger projects may take 3-4 weeks. I always provide a detailed timeline before starting any project.

Do you offer unlimited revisions?

Yes! I believe in getting your design perfect. All my packages include unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied with the final result. Your happiness with the design is my top priority.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive all the files you need for both digital and print use. This typically includes vector files (AI, EPS, SVG), high-resolution raster files (PNG, JPG), and any specific formats required for your use case.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and am comfortable with remote collaboration across different time zones. Communication is primarily through email, WhatsApp, and video calls as needed.

What's your payment process?

For smaller projects (under $50), I require full payment upfront. For larger projects, I typically ask for 50% upfront and 50% upon completion. I accept payments through PayPal, bank transfer, and other secure payment methods.

Can you rush my project?

Yes, I offer rush delivery for urgent projects. Rush orders typically incur a 50% surcharge and are subject to my current workload. Please contact me to discuss your timeline and I'll let you know if I can accommodate your deadline.

Do you provide brand guidelines?

Yes! Brand identity packages include comprehensive brand guidelines that cover logo usage, color codes, typography, spacing requirements, and do's and don'ts. This ensures consistent brand application across all materials.

What if I don't like the initial concepts?

No worries! If the initial concepts don't match your vision, we'll have a detailed discussion about your preferences and I'll create new concepts based on your feedback. This process continues until we find the perfect direction.

Do you offer ongoing design support?

Yes, I'm happy to provide ongoing design support for my clients. Whether you need additional marketing materials, social media graphics, or updates to existing designs, I'm here to help your brand grow.

Can you match my existing brand style?

Absolutely! If you have existing brand elements that you'd like me to work with, I can create new designs that complement and enhance your current brand identity while maintaining consistency.

Still Have Questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? I'm here to help! Feel free to reach out and I'll get back to you within 24 hours.
